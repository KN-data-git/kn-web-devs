How we work

Research-first

Every campaign starts with data. We don't pitch creative — we pitch understanding. If the data doesn't support it, we don't ship it.

Small and intentional

We're a tight team where everyone shapes the work directly. No layers of approval. If you're here, your opinion matters from day one.

Write what you learn

Knowledge that stays in someone's head doesn't compound. We publish our thinking — internally and externally — because that's how teams get smarter.

Seoul + Remote

Our core team is in Seoul, but we work with collaborators across time zones. What matters is the quality of thinking, not where it happens.
